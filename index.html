SENSOR PARAMETERISATION

Automatic IMU calibration for a carousel

Developed a calibration method for the carousel's MPU6050 IMU. It estimated offsets and five alignment parameters for the three-axis gyroscope and accelerometer, reconciled sensor, carousel, and global coordinate frames, and used gravity and radial acceleration as physical references for calibrated operation after restart.

COMPUTER VISION

Ellipse detection with the Hough transform

Built an RGB-camera pipeline for a robotics target-detection task: grayscale and blur, thresholding, Canny edges, morphological masking, line-based centre estimation, and a five-parameter ellipse Hough accumulator. Min/max axis constraints, ratio checks, and circumference normalisation improved candidate scoring under noise and incomplete contours.
